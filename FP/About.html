About Me

I am Victoria and I am a Student

Hi! Welcome to my personal portfolio page.

I am currently a junior double majoring in Business Administration and Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University. I am passionate about the intersection between business and technology and wish to pursue a career path in Consulting or Product Management. I think my biggest strength is my ability to connect people from diverse backgrounds together. I grew up in China and came to the U.S for high school and college and I just find myself really enjoying talking with people from diverse cultural background and learn about their stories.

To leverage my strengths and practice my leadership skills, I am actively involved in many leadership positions on campus. I serve as VP Finance for Chinese Student Association where I am responsible for seeking sponors for funding and other organizations for partnership. I am also Vice President for CMU Summit where we invite alumins from different industries to talk to current students about their career journey and industry insights. Apart from these, I was also able to step outside of my comfort zone and become one of the few international students in the school's premium business co-ed fraternity Alpha Kappa Psi where I met similar minded peers.

This summer, I will be working as a Business Product Analyst intern at Santander Bank where I will assist the Digital Transformation team to better their online banking platform. I am really excited about this opportunity and can't wait for summer to start!
